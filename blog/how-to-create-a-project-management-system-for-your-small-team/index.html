By Dr. Connor Robertson | 2025-07-08

Process improvement does not mean starting from scratch. It means looking at how work actually gets done and finding ways to make it better. At Elixir Consulting Group, Dr. Connor Robertson works alongside teams to identify improvements that save time, reduce errors, and increase quality.

In this article, we break down how to create a project management system for your small team. These are the strategies we use with clients at Elixir Consulting Group, and they are designed to be practical, actionable, and applicable to businesses of any size.
